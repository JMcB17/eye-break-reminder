Take a break!

Spend few seconds now, for a healthier future.

Close your eyes, for a moment!

Your eyes are probably getting dried by constant exposure to the monitor. Just close your eyes for few seconds which will help to clean and ease your eyes.


At a minimum, slowly blink your eyes few times.

Move your eyes in vertical, horizontal or circular motion.


Move your eyes to your left end. Then slowly move it to the center and then to the right. Make sure that you are not moving your head and the movement of your eye is slow, while doing this. The same can be done in the vertical motion, by looking up, then straight and then down.


Attempt a similar movement by assuming the left, bottom, right and top as points of a circle and tracking them in a circular fashion.

20-20-20 Rule


As per this rule, you are expected to take 20 seconds break in every 20 minutes. During this time, look at any object placed at 20 feet away from you.


Look at the farthest point that you can see from where you are sitting OR look out the window, at something pleasant!

Now, something for your body!

If possible, please walk around for a minute and come back. Your back is already hurting (you probably don't know it yet). At a minimum, move your body to the left and right while sitting in the chair, holding at your chair handle. Move your neck to your left and right too.
